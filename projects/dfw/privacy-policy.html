DFW Privacy Policy

DFW, also known as Down For Workouts, is a local-only exercise tracking app. It is designed to work without an account, backend service, advertising, analytics, or third-party tracking.

Information We Collect

DFW does not collect personal information.

The app does not ask for your name, email address, location, contacts, health records, profile information, or account details.

DFW does not use analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, tracking pixels, or third-party data collection tools.

Information Stored On Your Device

DFW stores app data locally on your device so the app can work properly. This may include:

  • daily rep targets
  • banked reps
  • completion history
  • streak and momentum state
  • rest-day settings
  • reveal and reset times
  • notification preference
  • unlocked facts
  • app settings

This information stays on your device. It is not sent to us or to a backend server.

Notifications

If you choose to turn on reminders, DFW asks Android for notification permission.

Notifications are used only to send local reminders at your chosen reveal time. You can turn reminders off in the app’s Settings or in your device settings.

DFW may use boot-completed access so scheduled local reminders can be restored after your phone restarts.

Haptics

DFW may use device vibration for small haptic responses during app interactions, such as the fact reveal flow.

Feedback By Email

If you choose to send feedback by email, your email app will open and you decide what to send.

If you email us, we may receive your email address and whatever information you include in the message. We use that only to read, respond to, and improve DFW.

Data Sharing

DFW does not sell, rent, or share personal data.

Because DFW does not collect personal data through the app, there is no app-collected personal data to share with third parties.

Data Deletion

You can delete your DFW history from Settings inside the app.

You can also delete app data by uninstalling DFW from your device. Uninstalling removes locally stored app data according to your device’s normal app removal process.

Children’s Privacy

DFW is not directed at children. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Health And Safety

DFW is a reps target and tracking app. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or a prescribed exercise program.

You choose the movements you perform. Exercise within your limits and speak with a GP, doctor, or qualified health professional before exercising if you have any concerns.

Changes To This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above.
